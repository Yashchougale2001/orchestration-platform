# Incident Response

Report incidents immediately, follow steps to isolate, investigate, and remediate threats.
1. Incident Classification
Severity	Examples	Response Time
Critical	Active breach, ransomware	< 15 minutes
High	Malware, compromised accounts	< 1 hour
Medium	Phishing attempts, suspicious activity	< 4 hours
Low	Failed logins, spam	< 24 hours
2. Reporting
Email: security@company.com
Phone: Security Hotline (ext. 5555)
Portal: IT Service Desk
Report immediately—do not wait for confirmation.

3. Response Phases
Detection – Confirm and classify the incident
Containment – Isolate affected systems and accounts
Eradication – Remove threats and patch vulnerabilities
Recovery – Restore systems from clean backups
Review – Document lessons learned within 5 days
4. Key Rules
Do not modify or delete potential evidence
Limit information to need-to-know personnel
Do not discuss incidents on social media
Cooperate fully with investigations
5. Escalation
Situation	Escalate To
Data breach confirmed	Legal, CISO
Critical systems affected	CIO, Executive Team
Criminal activity suspected	Legal, Law Enforcement
